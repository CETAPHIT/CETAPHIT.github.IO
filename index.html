Preface：

This dictionary has been created for those who need  up-to-date definitions of words that describe females and those interested in learning English words referring to females.This dictionary is intended to help international students learn about American culture and the different ways to describe women or girls. I am a international student, and students like me may be not familiar with the usage and interpretation of English words. So, I defined these English words by watching TV talk shows and TV dramas. I think these words related to females may helpful to the reader. For those who already know the meaning of the words, this dictionary is a great chance to look back, and also the reader may find a novel  meaning. This dictionary presents the definition, pronuanciation and some examples of the word. I would also put some pictures in this dictionary to add some fun.
